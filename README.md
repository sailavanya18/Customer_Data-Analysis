# 📊 Customer Data Analysis

## 🧠 Business Problem Statement

Organizations often work with large volumes of raw transactional data that cannot directly support decision-making. Without structured analysis, valuable business insights remain hidden.

This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior to understand how **gender, age groups, product categories, and payment methods** impact revenue and business performance. The goal is to convert raw data into meaningful insights that support strategic decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze shopping distribution according to **gender**
* Identify sales performance by **age groups**
* Study distribution of purchase categories relative to **gender and age**
* Analyze relationship between **payment methods and sales**
* Evaluate distribution of payment methods
* Build an interactive **Power BI dashboard**
* Derive business insights and recommendations

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

* **MySQL** – Data storage & analysis
* **SQL** – Data querying & aggregation
* **Power BI** – Data visualization & dashboard creation
* **PowerPoint** – Presentation & reporting

---

## 📂 Dataset Overview

Retail transactional dataset containing:

* Invoice Number
* Customer ID
* Gender
* Age
* Category
* Quantity
* Price
* Payment Method
* Invoice Date
* Shopping Mall

Data collected from multiple shopping malls (2021–2023).

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Customers
* Total Orders
* Total Revenue
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Total Quantity Sold

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Female customers contribute higher revenue.
* Age group **36–45** is the most valuable customer segment.
* Clothing and Shoes drive maximum sales.
* Cash is the most preferred payment method.
* January recorded the highest monthly revenue.
* Seasonal trends influence overall sales performance.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Target female customers through promotions and loyalty programs.
* Focus marketing efforts on the 36–45 age group.
* Introduce bundled offers in the Clothing category.
* Improve end-of-year marketing campaigns.
* Enhance billing efficiency for smoother checkout experience.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features (Power BI)

* Revenue by Gender
* Revenue by Age Group
* Category-wise Performance
* Payment Method Distribution
* Monthly Revenue Trends
* KPI Summary Cards
* Interactive Filters

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how **SQL and Power BI** can be effectively used to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights. The analysis supports data-driven decision-making, enhances customer targeting, and improves revenue growth strategies.
